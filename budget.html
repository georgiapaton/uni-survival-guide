Budget tips

How to save those £££'s

  1. Set yourself a budget
  2. Compare and see if you can save money on your Phone bill
  3. Sign up to student discount websites such as Unidays and StudentBeans
  4. Check out money saving websitses including SaveTheStudent, MoneySavingExpert, and StudentMoneySaver
  5. Find your local charity shops and shop second hand.
