Study Tips

Here are our top tips for better studying

  1. Make yourself a schedule, do that here on My study life link to your Uni 365 account, add in your lectures, exams and any tasks that you need to do.
  2. Set yourself mini deadlines for your assignments which can be done easily with this Assignment Survival Kit, you just fill in your dates and it does the rest for you!
  3. Dedicate a day for reading each week.
  4. Remember to relax do things that you enjoy and get some Headspace
